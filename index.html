Music Trackr

Your real Apple Music listening history — top songs, albums, artists, and total time, read straight from your iPhone.

Download on the App Store

Top everything

Your most-played songs, albums, artists, genres, and playlists — ranked by what you actually listen to.

Total listening time

See how much time you've really spent listening, plus skip counts and full per-item drill-downs.

Shareable cards

Turn your top songs or albums into a beautiful card to share with friends or on social.

Free & on-device

No account, no sign-in. Free with a one-time in-app purchase to remove the banner ad.

Completely private

Music Trackr never uploads your listening data anywhere. Everything is read from your own music library and stays on your device. Your music history is yours alone.
